# TRUE VISION PROJECT — POSTER SERIES
**Limited Pre-Drop Release (June-July 2026)**

---

## PRODUCTION SPECS

### Paper & Printing
- **Format:** A2 (420 × 594 mm) or A3 (297 × 420 mm) — recommend A2 for impact
- **Paper stock:** 250gsm poster paper (natural white or cream #F5F3EE)
- **Ink:** Black (CMYK 0,0,0,100)
- **Finish:** Matte (no gloss)
- **Print method:** Lithographic or digital offset
- **Quantity per design:** 1,000 units each = 3,000 total posters
- **Turnaround:** 7-10 days from approval to arrival

### Design System
**Font family (all posters):**
- Headline: Cormorant Garamond (serif) — 72-96pt, medium weight
- Body/Label: Space Mono (monospace) — 12-18pt
- All text: Black #111111 or #000000 (solid black ink)

**Visual elements (all posters):**
- Corner marks: 20mm × 2px border at all four corners (document/cinema aesthetic)
- Grain overlay: Subtle texture across entire poster (2-3% opacity scan grain)
- Bleed: 5mm all sides
- Safe area: 10mm margins from edge (avoid corner marks)

**Color palette:**
- Background: White #FFFFFF or cream #F5F3EE
- Text: Black #111111
- NO color accents. NO gradients. Pure monochrome.

**Layout principles:**
- Centered composition or left-aligned (document style)
- Generous whitespace
- Maximum 2 font sizes per poster
- Minimal illustration — type-forward
