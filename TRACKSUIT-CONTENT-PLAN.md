## PHASE 2 — THE GAME (the moment the sample arrives — REAL footage only)

**The Find-The-Pocket Challenge — this is the viral engine:**
- Film real people (friends, gym, barbershop): hand them the tracksuit — "60 seconds. Find the pocket. If you find it, you keep [€20/the suit]." Film the search, the fails, the reveal. Every video is the same repeatable format.
- Every seeded creator (Lil Skag first) gets the same brief with their gifted set: "film someone trying to find the pocket."
- CTA on every video: `The pocket is hidden. So is the website. truevisionproject.com`
- This needs ZERO founder face if you don't want it — the product is the star, the hands can be anyone's.

**Rule from here:** AI clips only until the sample lands. The moment real product exists, REAL footage replaces AI everywhere. AI teases; reality sells.
