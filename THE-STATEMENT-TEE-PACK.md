## THE CONCEPT (one sentence)

**A heavyweight tee that says one thing — quiet in execution, loud in meaning. Minimal on the front. A statement on the back. A private line inside, only the wearer sees.**

Built from nothing. Worn by those who understand. The tee version.

The whole idea: it doesn't shout with graphics. It carries a *statement*. Someone who knows, knows. That restraint IS the flex — it matches everything TVP already is.

---

## THE DESIGN (exact, so there's no guessing)

### Garment
- **Style:** heavyweight boxy tee, slightly oversized, dropped shoulder (tracksuit-culture fit)
- **Weight:** 240–280 GSM (heavy, structured, premium drape — NOT a thin tee)
- **Fabric:** 100% combed ring-spun cotton, heavy jersey
- **Collar:** ribbed, structured (sits well, doesn't curl)
- **Colourways:** **Black** (`#111111`) and **Bone / Cream** (`#F5F3EE`) — the two TVP colours, nothing else
- **Sizes:** S / M / L / XL (add XXL if supplier allows)

### The print — three placements

**1. FRONT — minimal.**
Small TVP "V" mark, left chest, ~8cm tall. Tonal or single-colour (subtle). That's it. The front whispers.

**2. BACK — the statement.**
One line, clean, across the upper back (shoulder-blade height), readable but not graffiti-loud.
- Typography: **Space Mono, uppercase** (the brand's technical/archive voice)
- Colour: monochrome contrast — off-white print on the black tee, black print on the cream tee
- **The line (pick one — my pick is #1):**
  1. **BUILT FROM NOTHING** ← recommended. Short, powerful, it's the brand's soul.
  2. NEVER FORGET WHERE YOU CAME FROM
  3. WORN BY THOSE WHO UNDERSTAND

**3. INSIDE — the private detail (the special touch).**
Printed inside the back neck (or inner hem): **"NEVER FORGET WHERE YOU CAME FROM"** — small, only the wearer sees it. A message to *them*, not the world. (This mirrors the tracksuit's hidden-pocket idea — a secret, considered detail. It's the thing that makes it feel personal and premium.)

**Optional:** small woven tag — "TVP // 1 OF [run size]" — numbered, reinforces scarcity.

### Print method
Screen print, matte finish (durable, clean). Keep it flat and tonal — no glossy plastisol shine. The minimal feel depends on a soft, matte print.

## PRODUCTION

- **Source:** heavyweight blanks from a streetwear blank supplier (e.g. AS Colour, Stanley/Stella heavy line) OR through your cap supplier's network. Then local/print-on-demand screen printing.
- **Sample FIRST:** 1 sample per colourway. Check fit, weight, print placement, wash test. Never run without holding the sample.
- **Run size at launch:** small + limited — **50 to 100 units**, numbered, no restock.
- **Cost estimate:** blank €6–10 + print €2–4 = **~€8–14 per unit**.
