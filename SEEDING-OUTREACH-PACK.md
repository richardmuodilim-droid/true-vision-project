## THE RULES (read before sending anything)

1. **Never send a template blast.** Every first DM must reference something specific and real about THEM (a song, a post, a fight, a moment). One personalized DM beats ten generic ones.
2. **Give without asking.** The gift comes with zero obligation stated. The ask ("film it if you feel it") comes only AFTER they say yes.
3. **Small is the target.** 1,000–20,000 followers. Big accounts ignore you; small accounts remember who believed in them early. That's literally the brand's message.
4. **One follow-up maximum,** 5–7 days later. Silence after that = move on, no hard feelings.
5. **Track everything** in the table at the bottom.

## WHO TO TARGET (the profile)

People who ARE the brand's story — talented, hungry, from places the industry ignores:
rappers and singers on the way up, boxers and MMA fighters from local gyms, barbers with a following, League of Ireland / lower-league footballers, fitness creators who train in council-estate gyms, students building something. Ireland and Lombardy first — this is a local-first brand.
