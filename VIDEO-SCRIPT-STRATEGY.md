# TRUE VISION PROJECT — VIDEO SCRIPT & CONTENT STRATEGY
**Foundation Video: "This Is True Vision Project"**

---

## THE VIDEO SCRIPT (FINAL)

**Duration:** 60 seconds  
**Platform:** Instagram Reels, TikTok, YouTube Shorts  
**Purpose:** Foundation video. Introduce what TVP really is. Build anticipation for what's coming.  
**Tone:** Intense, real, direct. Not corporate. Not sales-y. Just truth.

---

### SCRIPT (Ready to Film)

```
[OPEN: Founder, intense, real, direct to camera]

"Most brands are built for people who don't exist.

They're made for a fantasy.

But you're real.

[PAUSE — let it land]

Your struggle is real. Your background is real. 
The way you see the world — that's real.

And yet, nobody builds FOR you.

[LEAN IN — closer to camera]

We decided that had to change.

So we created True Vision Project.

Not to sell you something.

To represent YOU.

[BEAT — step back, confident]

Because representation isn't about products.

It's about saying: You matter. Your story matters.
The people who come from where you come from — you're not alone.

[STAND UP, DIRECT EYE CONTACT]

We're building something different here.

Something that actually SEES you.

And if that resonates — if you're ready to be part of something real —

Share this. 

Bring your people.

Because what comes next — it's bigger than all of us.

[HOLD FOR 2 SECONDS — serious, confident]

And we're only building it if YOU'RE in.

That's True Vision Project.

[CLOSE]
```

---

## HOW TO FILM THIS

### Camera & Setup
- **Location:** Simple background (brick wall, white wall, natural light preferred)
- **Camera:** Phone camera (vertical for Reels/TikTok, horizontal for YouTube Shorts)
- **Audio:** Clear, no background noise
- **Lighting:** Natural daylight or ring light. Face well-lit.

### Direction & Performance
- **Energy:** Starts serious, builds intensity, ends solid and confident
- **Eyes:** Direct to camera the entire time. This is a conversation.
- **Movement:** 
  - Start: Standing still, serious
  - Middle: Lean in at "We decided that had to change"
  - End: Stand up at "We're building something different"
- **Pace:** Slow. Don't rush. Let pauses land. This is about feeling, not speed.
- **Emotion:** Real. Not acted. Like you're telling a friend something that matters.

### Pauses & Emphasis
- **[PAUSE]** after "But you're real" — let people sit with it (2 seconds)
- **[BEAT]** after "To represent YOU" — moment of truth (1.5 seconds)
- **[HOLD]** at end for 2 full seconds — confidence, not rushed

## WHAT THIS VIDEO DOES

### The Problem (First 15 seconds)
"Most brands are built for people who don't exist."

**What it does:** Makes people feel SEEN. They watch and think: "Yes. That's me. Nobody builds for people like me."

**Emotional hit:** Validation that they're not crazy for feeling invisible.

### The Solution (Next 20 seconds)
"We created True Vision Project. Not to sell you something. To represent YOU."

**What it does:** Introduces TVP not as a product, but as a movement that actually sees them.

**Emotional hit:** Hope. Relief. "Finally."

### The Call (Last 25 seconds)
"Share this. Bring your people. What comes next is bigger than all of us. And we're only building it if YOU'RE in."

**What it does:** Makes them feel NEEDED. Not like customers, but like founders.

**Emotional hit:** Belonging. Responsibility. Want to be part of something.

## WHY THIS WORKS

✅ **Doesn't sell anything** — No product mention. No pricing. No "buy now."  
✅ **Solves a real problem** — Addresses invisibility + lack of representation  
✅ **Makes people feel SEEN** — "Your struggle is real. Your background is real."  
✅ **Creates belonging** — "You're not alone. We're building this together."  
✅ **Builds anticipation** — "What comes next is bigger than all of us."  
✅ **Calls to action naturally** — Share because it matters, not because we asked  
✅ **Original language** — No clichés, no corporate BS, no repetition  
✅ **Hits hard in 60 seconds** — Every word matters
